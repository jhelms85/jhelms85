Website Evaluations

Zillow

This website is a real estate and rental listing service.

CRAP Principles:

  • Contrast
  • Repitition
  • Alignment
  • Proximity

The website follows all of these principles. Everything is very well aligned, and the proximity of images, buttons make sense and there is room for everything to breathe. The contrast, although minimal and simple, is well thought out. Everything is easy to read, and not busy and distracting.

Yale School of Art

This is the website for the Yale School of Art graduate school.

CRAP Principles:

  • Contrast
  • Repitition
  • Alignment
  • Proximity

This website does not follow any of the CRAP principles. The contrast of the site is bad, text has weird backgrounds and colors. Nothing is consistently styled, things are not aligned properly, and even the proximity of elements is inconsistent and weird.
